Daily targets
  • 2,000–2,200 caloriesA real deficit at your size — nearly double the winter crash-diet intake
  • 185g+ proteinThis is what makes the loss come from fat instead of muscle
  • Floor: never under 1,800Two days under the floor in a week = flag it at Monday check-in
  • Elliptical: 45 min max per session, 4 sessions max per weekLifting first — elliptical after or on off days
  • Weigh every morningJudge only the 7-day average. Single days mean nothing.

Weekends
  • Breakfast — the griddle slotButtermilk Waffles, Cinnamon Roll Pancakes, or a hot oatmeal bowl. Pick per morning.
  • Dinner — the flex mealFamily plans, going out, grilling. One weekend dinner can be whatever, guilt-free — the week is already locked.

Lift split — Month 1
3 days per week, 35–40 minutes. Every exercise rated 4 or 5. No leg day in Month 1 — habit before optimization.
Day A — Chest + Biceps
Dumbbell Bench Press3 × 8–12
Incline Dumbbell Press3 × 8–12
Dumbbell Fly2 × 12–15
Standing Dumbbell Curl3 × 10–12
Hammer Curl2 × 10–12
Day B — Shoulders + Triceps
Arnold Press3 × 8–12
Machine Chest Press3 × 8–12
Dumbbell Lateral Raise3 × 12–15
Rope Pushdown3 × 10–12
Cable Pushdown (bar)2 × 12–15
Day C — Arms + Pump
Preacher Curl3 × 10–12
Incline Dumbbell Curl2 × 10–12
Cable Curl2 × 12–15
Concentration Curl2 × 12–15
Chest movement of choice (any Day A press)3 × 8–12
  • Rest 90 sec – 2 min between sets
  • Progression: top of the rep range on all sets = add 5 lbs next session
  • Elliptical after lifting is optional — 20–30 min, counts against the weekly cap of 4
What layers in later
  • Month 2 (mid-Aug): back work you rated 3 — Lat Pulldown, Seated Cable Row, Face Pulls — added to Day B and C
  • Month 3 (mid-Sep): micro-dosed legs — Walking Lunges + Standing Calf Raise as finishers, 2 days per week. Legs need to be under you before goalie reps start Oct 29.
