Daily targets
  • 2,000–2,200 caloriesA real deficit at your size — nearly double the winter crash-diet intake
  • 185g+ proteinThis is what makes the loss come from fat instead of muscle
  • Floor: never under 1,800Two days under the floor in a week = flag it at Friday check-in
  • Elliptical: 45 min max per session, 4 sessions max per weekLifting first — elliptical after or on off days
  • Weigh every morningJudge only the 7-day average. Single days mean nothing.

  • Month 3 (mid-Sep): micro-dosed legs — Walking Lunges + Standing Calf Raise as finishers, 2 days per week. Legs need to be under you before goalie reps start Oct 29.

Friday check-in
  • Report four things: 7-day average weight · lifts completed (target 3) · protein average · one flag if anything's slipping
  • Two stalled weeks = we adjust togetherYou don't slash calories solo. That's the pattern interrupt.
  • Under the 1,800 floor twice in a week = same ruleUnder-eating is the old engine. Flag it, don't ride it.

Week 1 lift cards — finding your weights
This week's job is calibration, not effort. On every exercise: start lighter than you think, do a set of 10, and add weight until the last 2 reps are genuinely hard. That's your working weight — log it. Week 2 starts progression from there.
